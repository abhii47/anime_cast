Mikey Manjiro Sano

Dark impulse Mikey is Manjiro Sano (佐野万次郎), but was also known as Mikey. He is the deuteragonist of the Manga/Anime Tokyo Revengers.He is Manjiro Sano fallen into darkness and controlled by "Dark Impulse" after losing and killing everyone he loved.

Abilities

  • Martial Arts
  • Nuclear Kick
  • Charisma
  • Disarticulation
  • Dark Impulse
  • Weapon Mastery
  • Hypnosis/Influence

    • Backstory

    Mikey during the Toman (Tokyo Manji) Vs Kanto Manji gang war, explains that the reason he is moving away is because of his "Dark Impulse" which forces him to kill anyone in front of him, Takemichi Hanagaki trying to save Mikey , asks him to release Dark Impulse, a type of Curse that makes Mikey more powerful, cold and psychopathic to the point of laughing while attacking Takemichi with a Katana.

    Personality

    Mikey was a carefree teenager who loved pulling pranks on those he considered his closest friends. He cared a lot about them and was determined to protect their honor. However, because of Kisaki Tetta and his Dark Impulse curse, he lost Shinichiro (his older brother), Keisuke Baji (one of his best friends), Izana and Emma ( his half-brothers) Ken Ryuguji/Draken (one of his best friends), as time passes he slowly begins to embrace his Dark Impulse because of his losses, he begins to move away from his friends to protect them but they they go after Mikey to save him, before the Tokyo Manji vs Kanto Manji war, he had killed South Terano (a guy who had something similar to Mikey's Dark Impulse) during the war of the three deities, showing how cold Dark Impulse Mikey is, after his Bad Ending, he is empty, angry and depressed after killing all of Kanto Manji and Tokyo Manji, he always thinks about the massacre his curse caused and starts crying, when his Dark Impulse curse is in control he becomes completely insane and sadistic to kill anyone in front of him and especially to make Mikey himself suffer more and more, When "Dark Impulse" begins to influence him, Dark Impulse Mikey becomes more aggressive, sadistic and crazy, he will simply kill anything in his path, but in this state, he is still able to speak normally and even manages to "restrain" a little desire to kill, but this gets even worse when "Dark Impulse" completely takes control, Dark Impulse Mikey becomes even more insane and psychotic, he laughs like a madman when he kills people and his enemies, in this state of "Full Dark Impulse ", he becomes so crazy that he ends up losing the ability to speak and just laughs maniacally while killing people and his opponents

    Appearance

    Dark Impulse Mikey is a teenager of below average height, with piercing black eyes and long blonde hair tied only at the top, with the hair on the sides moving freely, he usually wears a black shirt or black shirt with long sleeves, white beige pants and black boots with some white details, when "Dark Impulse" starts to influence/control him, he has white eyes and a sadistic smile
